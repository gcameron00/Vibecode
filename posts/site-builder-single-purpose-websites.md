My wife was having problems with a QR Code she had generated for accepting payments to her bank account. Rather than trying to trouble shoot, I looked for another site to generate the QR Codes. Didn't find one immediately, but did notice the standard had changed recently. 

So quickly opened up [Site Builder](https://allaboutthevibes.blog/project/?slug=site-builder), gave it a quick few paragraph specification, mentioned the new standard and clicked submit.

I was just pulling into Zurich station at the time. I changed train, ordered a beer in the restuarant car, sat down, reopened my iPad, and there was a working QR Code generating site waiting for me.

Feel free to use it, everything is done locally - https://qrcode4payment.pages.dev
