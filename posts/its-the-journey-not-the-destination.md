At least, that's what I'm needing to tell myself.

Take this blog for example. Did I need to code with AI to put it into existence. Did I already have a place to blog, did I already have a hosting agreement that would have let me add this domain for free? Er, yes.

So why bring a pretty basic content management system to life?

> It’s the journey, not the destination

I hope I'm enjoying the view and learning on the way, because this is certainly not the shortest route. I suspect there are many pitfalls to navigate along the way too.

Currently, the journey is fun!
