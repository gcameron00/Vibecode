A couple of current projects have involved asking Claude to add images. Yes, I realise there are many other ways of getting image assets, but this is my journey to see what works and what doesn’t.

In one case, I’ve been seeing how far I can get with a web-based game. Claude did a reasonable job of coming up with retro-looking players and baddies, but when I was trying to get a splash screen in the style of an arcade game, I was getting nowhere.

Similarly, on this blog, I wanted a logo in the form of the waveform V. It took a number of iterations before I got something I considered reasonable.

My suspicion is that Claude is looking to use code to generate vector images, rather than falling back on just creating raster images when it would work equally well.
