Started what feels like a more meaningful project. The Money is meant to provide an overview of a number of standard household financial products.

It was triggered by wanting to understand spend on credit cards, so that is where I have started with the implementation. However, it is also good to have everything in one place.

I know there are a number of aggregator sites but they rely on Open Banking offerings and I don't think Switzerland has mandated them yet. Also this way I get to define the feature set.

Site security is enforced by Cloudflare, not by my vibecode!
