This project is more a collection of mini-projects around a theme. The theme being a 64 by 64 grid of pixels. Boards like this exist in reallife, in fact I've considered both buying a board or buying products based on the boards.

Bringing this set of options to life was a lot of fun, and I'm sure I could come up with another 50 mini-ideas of what to display.
