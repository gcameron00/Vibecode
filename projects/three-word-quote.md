I was inspired to start this project when reading about the American poet Waldo Emerson. While jumping around tangentally through links I came across the idea of a three word quote and people using them on posters.

The then site came together very quickly. I wanted it to be a simple as possible with very little to distract from the message in the words. I steered the design of the backgrounds to feel like nature and then iterated once to get slightly more going on.

The mountains reflecting on the lake is the most complex of the designs and even that does as good job of not being distracting.
